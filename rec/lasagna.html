Lasagna

Description

Yummy lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is known for its rich and comforting flavors. It is typically made by layering flat pasta sheets with a combination of ingredients such as ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, tomato sauce, ground meat (like beef or pork), and sometimes vegetables like spinach or mushrooms.

The pasta sheets are often pre-cooked or layered with a sauce to ensure they cook properly in the oven. Each layer is assembled in a baking dish, creating a hearty and substantial casserole. The top layer is usually finished with a generous sprinkle of cheese to create a golden, bubbly crust when baked.

The flavors of the ingredients meld together during baking, creating a harmonious combination of creamy, cheesy, meaty, and tomatoey goodness. The dish is then served in squares, allowing each bite to contain a delightful mixture of flavors and textures.

Yummy lasagna is often accompanied by a side salad and garlic bread, making it a popular and satisfying meal for gatherings or family dinners. It's a versatile dish that can be customized to suit individual tastes, and it's beloved by many for its comforting and indulgent qualities.

Ingredients:

  • - 9 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or cooked according to package instructions)
  • - 1 pound (450g) ground beef (or a combination of beef and pork)
  • - 1 onion, finely chopped
  • - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • - 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • - 2 (6-ounce) cans tomato paste
  • - 2 (6.5-ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • - 1/2 cup water
  • - 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • - 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • - 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • - 1 tablespoon salt
  • - 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • - 4 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • - 12 ounces ricotta cheese
  • - 1 egg
  • - 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • - 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • - 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground meat over medium-high heat until evenly browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Add sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, remaining parsley, and 3/4 teaspoon salt. Mix well and set aside.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange a single layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread with one-half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon a third of the meat sauce over the cheese. Repeat layers, ending with the sauce.
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil (make sure it doesn't touch the cheese) and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving to allow the lasagna to set.
Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

Note: Feel free to customize this recipe by adding vegetables like spinach or mushrooms, or by using a different combination of meats. You can also adjust the seasonings to suit your taste preferences.
