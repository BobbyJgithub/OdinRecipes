Cookies

Description

Cookies are delightful sweet treats loved by people of all ages. They come in various flavors, textures, and shapes, making them a versatile dessert option for any occasion. Whether you prefer soft and chewy or crisp and buttery, there's a cookie recipe for everyone.

Ingredients:

For Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies:

  • - 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • - 1 cup granulated sugar
  • - 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • - 2 large eggs
  • - 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • - 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • - 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • - 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • - 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

For Additional Variations:

  • - Additions like nuts, dried fruits, or other types of chocolate (white, dark, etc.) as per your preference

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the butter and sugar mixture, mixing until just combined.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips (and any additional add-ins like nuts or dried fruits if desired).
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still appear slightly soft.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Store in an airtight container for up to a week (if they last that long!).

Enjoy your freshly baked cookies!

Feel free to get creative with your cookie variations by adding your favorite mix-ins. Whether it's nuts, dried fruits, or different types of chocolate, customizing your cookies is part of the fun!
