Pretzels

Description

Pretzels are a classic baked snack known for their twisted knot shape and distinctive flavor. They are soft on the inside with a slightly crisp exterior and a delightful touch of salt. Pretzels are a favorite at parties, gatherings, or simply as a satisfying treat.

Ingredients:

For the Pretzel Dough:

  • - 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • - 1 packet active dry yeast
  • - 1 teaspoon salt
  • - 1 tablespoon sugar
  • - 4 cups all-purpose flour

For Boiling and Topping:

  • - 10 cups water
  • - 2/3 cup baking soda
  • - Coarse sea salt for sprinkling

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and salt. Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture and let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, add the flour and pour in the yeast mixture. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. In a large pot, bring the 10 cups of water and baking soda to a rolling boil.
  7. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a long rope. Twist the rope into a pretzel shape.
  8. Place the pretzels, one at a time, into the boiling water for about 30 seconds. Remove them with a slotted spoon and place them on the prepared baking sheets.
  9. Sprinkle the pretzels with coarse sea salt while they are still wet from the water bath.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the pretzels are golden brown.
  11. Allow the pretzels to cool slightly before serving.

Enjoy your freshly baked pretzels!

Feel free to get creative with your pretzel shapes or try different toppings like cinnamon sugar, shredded cheese, or even chocolate drizzle for a unique twist!
